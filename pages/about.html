Welcome to Back to 80's

Where nostalgia meets innovation. Explore the story of our journey and join us in celebrating the golden age of retro media.

Our Story

Back in 2025, a passionate entrepreneur, Daniyar, set out to create something unique. Inspired by his love for classic cinema and retro technology, he envisioned a space where people could relive the magic of the past through carefully curated VHS and audiocassette bundles.

Today, Back to 80’s stands as a celebration of timeless classics, offering an immersive experience for all lovers of retro culture.

Why Choose Us?

  • Carefully curated VHS and audiocassette bundles featuring iconic movies and soundtracks.
  • Authentic 80’s-inspired interiors for a truly nostalgic experience.
  • A community for collectors, enthusiasts, and newcomers alike.

Our Vision

We believe in preserving the magic of analog media while introducing it to new generations. From the first spin of an audiocassette to the click of a VHS tape, Back to 80's is here to keep the retro revolution alive.
